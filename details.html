Mercury

Mercury is the smallest planet and closest to the Sun. It has extreme temperatures and no atmosphere.

Venus

Venus is covered in thick clouds of acid and is hotter than any other planet.

Earth

Earth is the only known planet with life, liquid water, and a breathable atmosphere.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its iron-rich soil.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet with a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its beautiful rings made of ice and rock.

Uranus

Uranus rotates on its side, making its seasons extremely unusual.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and has extremely strong winds.
